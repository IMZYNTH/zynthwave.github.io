ZYNTH

I became heavily interested in livestreaming when I was nearing the end of my enlistment in the USAF, and eventually took the leap to press record for the first time around January of 2020. As I began getting more and more comfortable with talking and interacting with my viewers, I began to design overlays and other channel branding to try and set myself apart from other channels online.

Now that I'm a content creator, my videos on YouTube and Twitch are where I get to be myself and have fun with my friends and viewers. I needed a solid visual identity if I was going to be taken seriously in platforms that are already VERY saturated with personalities trying to become famous and well known.
