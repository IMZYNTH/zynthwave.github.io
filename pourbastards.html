POUR BASTARD'S BREWING

This whole company was created as an assignment to create branding for a bottle. The basic assignment requirements were to create three different flavors of whatever drink you're choosing. I took this as an opportunity to go buckwild with illustration and create a whole entity with MY personality.

This is one of the few projects that has been continuously revised throughout my time at Portland State University. Because of this, I feel as though it has adapted and grown along with me in the personality it communicates.
