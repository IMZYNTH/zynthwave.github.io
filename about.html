Hello!

My name is Johnathan, I’m a USAF Veteran currently going through the Graphic Design program at Portland State University pursuing my Bachelor’s Degree. I specialize in motion design and illustration, making sure that everything I create stays true to the voice and personality to which it belongs.

Towards the end of 2019 when I was getting ready to leave the Air Force, I started livestreaming on Twitch.com and discovered my desire to create designs that others and I could use in the digital space of content creation. I later started developing these skills and applying them to animation design for channels on Twitch and YouTube to use in their videos.

While pursuing my degree at PSU I have completed various passion projects as well as a few one-off projects such as department designs, shirts, and patches for clients at Legacy Health System. I've also created menu designs and branding for Harvester Taproom in St. Paul, Oregon. No matter if it’s for a personal project of mine, or contractual work for clients, I’m always happy to delve into the creative process in any format.
