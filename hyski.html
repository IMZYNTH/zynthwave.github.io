HYSKI PROPERTY SOLUTIONS

I had the opportunity to design a comprehensive visual identity for a general contracting company based in Oregon, including logo & wordmark design, branding materials, and promotional assets. My first and proudest step into the world of freelance design.

I've worked with this company to develop a visual identity and branding system from the ground up. The client expressed the need for a logo, wordmark, and cohesive business cards. Hyski is an old Norse word meaning household or family, the word is more commonly used to refer to said household as lowly or poor.
