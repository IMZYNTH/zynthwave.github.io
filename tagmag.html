TAGMAG

For my university thesis project, I tasked myself with designing a product that would not only bring a community closer, but invite others to gain knowledge and awareness to a prominent conflict in urban areas, Graffiti. This publication is a platform for graffiti writers and street artists to gain more exposure, and a way for beginners to become more familiar with the realm of urban art. This magazine contains interviews from local artists, themed photo collages, street photography, tag spotlights, and a tag of the month for every issue.

I started my journey by choosing street art & graffiti as a topic. I've never been a member of this community of artists, but I figured that my interest in it was enough of a spark to ignite a project of this magnitude.
